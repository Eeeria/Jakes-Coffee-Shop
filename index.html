Our Menu

Espresso

A concentrated coffee beverage brewed by forcing hot water under high pressure through finely ground coffee.

Cappuccino

A coffee-based drink made with espresso and steamed milk, topped with a layer of foamed milk.

Americano

A coffee drink prepared by diluting espresso with hot water, giving it a similar strength to drip coffee.

Latte

A classic espresso-based beverage that combines a shot of espresso with steamed milk and a small amount of foam. The latte offers a balanced and creamy flavor, making it a popular choice for coffee enthusiasts.

A refreshing blended coffee beverage with a smooth and creamy texture. It is made by combining espresso or instant coffee, milk, ice, and sugar, resulting in a delightful icy treat.

Takeaway Coffee

Enjoy the convenience of our delicious coffee on the go. Our takeaway coffee is carefully brewed to perfection, offering a rich and aromatic flavor that will satisfy your caffeine cravings wherever you are.

Iced Coffee

A chilled coffee drink perfect for hot days. Our iced coffee is made by pouring freshly brewed coffee over ice and optionally adding milk or a sweetener. It delivers a cooling sensation with a bold and invigorating taste.

Irish Coffee

A delightful blend of hot coffee, Irish whiskey, sugar, and topped with a layer of thick cream. This indulgent drink provides a warming and rich experience, perfect for those looking for a touch of luxury in their coffee.

Mocha

A decadent combination of espresso, steamed milk, chocolate syrup, and topped with whipped cream. The mocha offers a harmonious blend of coffee and chocolate flavors, creating a luscious and satisfying beverage.

Latte Macchiato

In a latte macchiato, the milk is carefully poured over the espresso, creating distinct layers of coffee and milk. It offers a smooth and creamy texture with a delicate balance of flavors.

Ristretto

Ristretto has a bold and robust flavor profile with less bitterness compared to a regular espresso.

Jobs

Barista

Baristas are the heart of a coffee shop. They are responsible for preparing and serving coffee and other beverages, such as espresso, cappuccinos, and lattes. They also take customer orders, operate espresso machines, and ensure the quality and consistency of the coffee.

Cashier

Cashiers handle customer transactions, process payments, and provide receipts. They may also take customer orders, answer questions, and provide information about menu items.

Server

Servers work in coffee shops that offer table service. They take customer orders, serve food and beverages, and ensure that customers have a pleasant dining experience. They may also assist with clearing tables and maintaining cleanliness in the dining area.
